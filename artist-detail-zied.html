Zied Gharsa

Zied Gharsa is a modern master of Malouf music, a classical genre rooted in Andalusian traditions. As the son of the iconic Kaddour Gharsa, he continues the family legacy with passion and precision.

Gharsa’s contributions include reviving rare Malouf compositions and introducing younger audiences to Tunisia’s classical music heritage.
