Sadak Thraya

Sadak Thraya is a respected voice in Tunisian popular music, recognized for his heartfelt lyrics and vocal authenticity.

His contributions helped shape the emotional tone of Tunisia’s chaabi and mezoued traditions, capturing the soul of everyday life.
