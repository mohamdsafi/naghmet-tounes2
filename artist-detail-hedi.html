Hédi Habbouba

Hédi Habbouba is one of Tunisia's most beloved figures in chaabi music. His spirited voice and authentic lyrics capture everyday life and cultural heritage.

Known for classics like "Ah ya lila" and "El Ghorba," Habbouba's performances remain etched in Tunisian musical memory.
