Mezoued

Mezoued is a vibrant and popular Tunisian music genre characterized by the use of the traditional bagpipe (mizwid), powerful rhythms, and heartfelt lyrics. It is often performed during celebrations and is deeply rooted in working-class traditions.

Notable Mezoued Artists

  • Hédi Habbouba

  • Samir lousif

  • Latifa Arfaoui
