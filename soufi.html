Soufi

Soufi music in Tunisia is a spiritual and devotional tradition rooted in Sufism, often performed in religious ceremonies and zawiyas. Featuring mystical poetry, repetitive rhythms, and meditative melodies, it aims to inspire divine connection and inner peace.

Notable Soufi Influences

  • Lotfi Bouchnak – known for integrating spiritual themes in his music.
  • Nour Arjoun – blends poetic mysticism with contemporary vocals.
