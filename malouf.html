Malouf

Malouf is a classical genre of Tunisian music with Andalusian origins, preserved and enriched through generations. Known for its intricate maqams, poetic lyrics, and refined performance, Malouf remains one of the most revered musical forms in Tunisia.

Notable Malouf Artists

  • Zied Gharsa
  • Mohamed Jammousi
  • Lotfi Bouchnak
