About Tunisian Music

Tunisia’s musical identity is shaped by centuries of intercultural exchange. Our sound blends Arab-Andalusian, Berber, African, and Mediterranean influences into a mosaic of rhythms and styles.

Malouf

The classical music of Tunisia, rooted in Andalusian traditions and refined by centuries of Tunisian craftsmanship.

Mezoued

A folk genre using bagpipes and percussion, often performed at celebrations and known for its raw emotional delivery.

Chaabi

Popular street music that carries the voice of the people — rhythmic, catchy, and rich in lyrical themes.

Soufi Music

Spiritual and trance-influenced, sufi music is deeply connected to Tunisian religious and poetic expression.
