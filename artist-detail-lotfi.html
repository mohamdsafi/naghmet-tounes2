Lotfi Bouchnak

Lotfi Bouchnak, often referred to as the "Pavarotti of the Arab world," is a Tunisian tenor, composer, and oud player known for blending classical Arabic music with Tunisian Malouf.

His contribution to preserving Arabic music while promoting modern interpretations has earned him international acclaim.
